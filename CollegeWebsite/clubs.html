Clubs

Art & Culture The Art Legion - established in 2017, The art legion is a group of enthusiastic artists cum engineers who like to do awesome stuff with color. They make cool wall paintings, fresh designs and more. Goonj The Rock Band - established way back in the early 00s, Goonj is IET’s official college band, and it performs in various cultural events of the college as well as participates in inter-college competitions. IET Literary Club (ILC)- organizes literary events in the campus, such as debate etc. Cultural Club - responsible for organizing various cultural events in the college like AAKSHANK - College Fest, INVENTO - Technical Fest and Engineers’ Day Celebrations among others. Social EWB IET Chapter - The college chapter for Engineers without Borders organization. NSS IET Chapter - The college chapter for National Service Scheme, Govt. of India. They organize various events like Blood Donation Camp, Plantation Drive, Cleanliness Drive etc. Note: There is no NCC unit in IET DAVV. Technical ACM IET Chapter - The college chapter for Association For Computing Machinery. It organizes talk shows and lectures on various software topics. E-Cell - It is the Entrepreneurship Cell. It promotes incubation, encourages startups and innovation. FeedBox - It is a club which focuses on Digital Marketing, Social Media and Graphic Design. ISHRAE IET Chapter - The college chapter for the Indian Society of Heating, Refrigerating and Air Conditioning Engineers. TEAM SAMANVAYA RACING (BAJA) - It is the team which competes in the BAJA SAE India event and also is dedicated to R&D in automotives.
